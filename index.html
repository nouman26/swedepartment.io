Chairman Message

Prof. Dr. Pardeep Kumar

Chairman

The main objective of the Software Engineering Department is to offer state of the art education to undergraduates so that they can define, design, develop, debug and deliver high quality, reliable and cost-effective software systems. Software engineers often work as part of a team working for computer systems design firms, software publishers, or for computer or electronic product manufacturers. Because of the wide variety of software applications, numerous opportunities can be pursued by software engineers.
